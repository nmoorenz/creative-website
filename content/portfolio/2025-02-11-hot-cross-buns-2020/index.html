Yarrows

Yarrows The Bakers
Spiced Fruit Easter Buns
Surprisingly weird name even though it makes total sense. They come with instructions on heating: 3-5 mins at 180C. Delicious, toasty, crunchy, fruity.
4/5 looking forward to more buns

Woolworths

Woolworths traditional hot cross buns
Moreish and delicious: tag line and good description. These are simple yet effective. Best value for money. 4/5 hot cross buns are essential services

Ima [homemade]

Ima Cuisine, $40 for 12 plus a couple hours of mixing and kneading and rising and baking. Delicious and so satisfying that I made them. 7/5 perfect score would make again
