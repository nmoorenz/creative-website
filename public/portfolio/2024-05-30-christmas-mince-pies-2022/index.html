Christmas Mince Pies 2022

Garvey’s Bakery mini mince pies 7pk Icing sugar topping, a little bit wholemeal crust, not so full with the filling, but they’re not even mini. 5/5 everything is delicious

Garvey’s Bakery Mince Pies, 6 for €5 No nonsense name, that’s for sure. Sugar coating on top! Not structurally sound, the top was thicker than the rest of the case. Full of filling, full of flavour. 5/5 everything is delicious

Mr Kipling Exceedingly Good Cakes 6 Deep Filled Mince Pies I can’t speak for Mr Kipling’s other cakes but these are good enough. It seems like Irish mince pie pastry isn’t blanched like NZ and it’s better for it. Tasty filling, nice design on top. 5/5 everything is delicious
